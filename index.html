Privacy Policy for LoveMe

Effective Date: July 20, 2025

LoveMe ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("LoveMe App"). Please read this policy carefully. By using the app, you consent to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal and non-personal data:

  • Authentication Data: Including email, phone number, and unique Firebase UID for login and account security (via Firebase Authentication).
  • Profile Information: Name, age, gender, profile photo, preferences, bio, and other voluntarily submitted information.
  • Chat and Messaging Data: Real-time chat messages, inbox history, match interactions (stored securely in Firebase Firestore).
  • Search & Matching Data: Search preferences, filters, and interactions with other users to personalize experience.
  • Support Queries: Messages or contact data submitted through support or feedback channels.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To provide personalized match recommendations
  • To allow secure messaging between users
  • To improve the functionality of the dashboard and search features
  • To respond to user support requests
  • To monitor usage and trends for analytics
  • To comply with legal obligations and platform policies

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third-party services to operate our app:

  • Firebase Authentication: For secure login and user management
  • Firebase Cloud Firestore: For secure, real-time database storage
  • Google Play Services: For core app functionality, compatibility, and analytics

These providers may collect technical data (such as device information and IP addresses) necessary to deliver their services. Their privacy policies govern how they handle that data.

4. Data Security

We implement strict security practices to safeguard your information:

  • All user authentication is handled via Firebase, which adheres to strong security protocols.
  • User data is encrypted during transmission (HTTPS/TLS) and securely stored in encrypted databases.
  • We restrict access to personal data to authorized personnel only.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct any inaccuracies in your personal data
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your account and associated data
  • Privacy Settings: Manage profile visibility and interaction preferences

To request account deletion or data access, please email us at support@lovemeapp.com or visit our Account Deletion Request Page.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with our legal obligations, and protect our legitimate interests. You can delete your data at any time by contacting support or through the app’s settings.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn that we have collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

8. Legal Compliance

We comply with applicable laws, including:

  • Google Play Developer Privacy Requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Union
  • Philippine Data Privacy Act (and similar international laws as applicable)

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, and we will notify you in-app where appropriate.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, or if you wish to exercise your privacy rights, please contact us:

  • Email: support@lovemeapp.com
  • Data Access & Deletion: https://yourdomain.com/delete-account
